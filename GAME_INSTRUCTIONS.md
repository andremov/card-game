# Buraco (Canastra) Card Game

## How to Play

### Game Setup
- 4 players divided into 2 teams (You + AI 2 vs AI 1 + AI 3)
- Two standard decks plus jokers (108 cards total)
- Each player starts with 11 cards
- Two buy piles of 11 cards each (taken when a player runs out of cards)

### Objective
Form melds (sets of 3+ cards of the same rank) and score points. The team with the highest score wins.

### Your Turn
1. **Draw Phase**: Click "DECK" to draw a card, or click "DISCARD" to take the entire discard pile (if you can form a meld with the top card)
2. **Meld Phase**:
   - Click cards in your hand to select them (they'll highlight in blue)
   - Click "Create Meld" to form a new meld (need at least 3 cards of same rank)
3. **Discard Phase**: Select one card and click "Discard" to end your turn

### Rules
- **Wildcards**: Jokers and 2s can substitute for any card
- **Clean Melds**: No wildcards (worth 500 bonus points when 7+ cards)
- **Dirty Melds**: Contains wildcards (worth 300 bonus points when 7+ cards)
- **Wildcard Limit**: Can't have more wildcards than natural cards in a meld
- **Taking Discard Pile**: You must be able to use the top card immediately in a meld

### Scoring
- Jokers: 50 points
- 2s: 20 points
- Aces: 15 points
- K, Q, J: 10 points each
- Other cards: 5 points each
- Canasta (7+ cards): +500 (clean) or +300 (dirty)
- Cards left in hand are subtracted from your score

### Controls
- **Click cards** to select/deselect them
- **DECK button**: Draw from deck
- **DISCARD button**: Take discard pile
- **Create Meld button**: Form new meld with selected cards
- **Discard button**: Discard selected card and end turn

### Tips
- Try to form melds quickly to reduce cards in hand
- Save wildcards for completing canastas
- Watch what opponents discard to avoid helping them
- AI opponents will play automatically after your turn
